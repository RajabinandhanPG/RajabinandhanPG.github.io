Operational Ownership

What the Role Covered

Scope of Responsibility

Acted as the sole technical owner for a healthcare organization covering home health, nursing, transport, medical equipment, and education businesses.

  • Supported 200+ internal users across five operating companies.
  • Closed 300+ tickets in roughly 8 months with a 98%+ close rate.
  • Reduced average IT response time by more than 80% using remote management and diagnostic workflows.
  • Owned endpoint operations, networking, websites, communications, security, and internal automations.

Cost and Resource Control

The role focused heavily on building with what already existed, localizing critical functions, and only adding tools where the gain was clear.

  • Saved at the upper end of $30K per month by avoiding unnecessary SaaS growth and consolidating operations into existing platforms.
  • Used internal automation and APIs instead of manual staffing increases for routine coordination work.
  • Designed systems that improved throughput without requiring vendor-heavy expansion.
Security

Governance, Hardening, and Incident Response

Localized and Compartmentalized Operations

Security work centered on controlling blast radius and standardizing operations in a regulated environment.

  • Standardized endpoint, identity, communication, and operational controls into localized and compartmentalized environments.
  • Aligned day-to-day practices with HIPAA and BAA requirements across devices, communications, and operational workflows.
  • Used centralized device, identity, and communication tooling to reduce drift and support repeatable governance.

Website Recovery and Hardening

One of the early priorities was stabilizing a repeatedly compromised production website.

  • Audited an actively compromised production site with long-running malware and persistent backdoors.
  • Removed malicious artifacts, patched vulnerabilities, and restored secure operations in under 72 hours.
  • Strengthened defenses to prevent reinfection and reduce future incident risk.
Infrastructure

Network, Endpoint, and Communications Systems

Device and Identity Management

Built a cross-platform management stack for rapid provisioning and support.

  • Used Action1, Windows provisioning packages, GCPW, Miradore, and Apple Business Manager.
  • Automated Google package installation and baseline setup for new systems.
  • Cut Windows deployment time by more than 80%.
Action1 GCPW Miradore Apple Business Manager

Network and Telephony Changes

Moved the organization away from fragile networking and carrier sprawl into a more controlled operating model.

  • Rebuilt network infrastructure around enterprise UniFi equipment and surveillance coverage.
  • Handled large-scale carrier and endpoint transitions across all five subcompanies.
  • Managed a footprint of 150+ workstations and 250+ phones and tablets.

Automation

Google API, Dialpad API, and Business Workflow Systems

Communication and Coordination Automations

  • Built Castor automations with Google APIs and the Dialpad API for synchronization, provisioning, and internal communication workflows.
  • Automated organizational email signature deployment with the Google Cloud Email API and custom HTML injection.
  • Synchronized field staff contact data and internal communication workflows through Dialpad integrations.
  • Built reminder and outreach flows to improve team coordination and customer communication.

Operational Systems and Growth Tooling

  • Engineered a public-facing review module with analytics across all five subcompanies.
  • Integrated NFC and QR employee ID workflows to support reviews and promotions.
  • Connected automated fax services directly into EMR-related operations.
  • Built a route-optimization workflow using AppSheet and Google Maps to reduce driving time and fuel cost.
