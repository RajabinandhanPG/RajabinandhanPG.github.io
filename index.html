About Me

Solutions Architect & Infrastructure Engineer

I architect and operate a dual-site infrastructure spanning Pollachi, Tamil Nadu (India) and Rockford, Illinois (USA) that supports internal business operations, client-hosted workloads, and a GPU-accelerated AI/ML platform.

The estate runs on three Proxmox hosts (Dell PowerEdge R740xd and R630), a Unraid NAS, and 10 segmented VLANs connected via WireGuard and UniFi Site Magic tunnels. The platform delivers 31 virtual machines, 4 LXC containers, 9 Docker application stacks, and 14 publicly exposed internal services — all managed through Docker/Portainer automation, Nginx Proxy Manager, and Cloudflare Tunnels.

Beyond day-to-day operations, I designed and deployed a local AI inference platform powered by an RTX 5090 (32GB VRAM) running ComfyUI with FLUX.2, Hunyuan3D 2.1, and TRELLIS2 models (~87GB model suite). I also built a distributed Blender rendering farm using Flamenco 3.9.
